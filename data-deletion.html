Data Deletion Instructions

Users who wish to delete their data from AnyJob may request removal by following the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like your profile and data removed from our platform, please send an email to mclarenmarco998@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."

What Information to Include

  • Your full name

Processing Time

We will process your request within 14 business days and confirm once the deletion is complete.
